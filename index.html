About

Physics-first simulation engineer

I'm a Physics graduate from Universitas Padjadjaran (UNPAD), Bandung, specializing in engineering modeling and simulation with COMSOL Multiphysics. My work spans acoustics, thermal behavior, structural mechanics, and fluid dynamics — multidisciplinary problems where the governing physics matters more than any single domain label.

What sets my work apart is the full R&D loop: I don't stop at colorful contour plots. My thesis simulation of a dental aerosol suction device was validated against physical measurements, and the structural bracket I analyzed in FEA was fabricated, installed, and used daily in the field. Simulation, to me, is a decision-making tool — it has to predict reality.

AcousticsThesisValidated

EOAS Acoustic Noise Reduction — Dental Aerosol Suction Device

FEM acoustic-parameter modeling of an Extraoral Aerosol Suction device that was producing 70–90 dB, far above the 65 dB clinical guideline. Screened materials via Transmission Loss and redesigned the geometry to cut noise.

70–90 dB → 55–73 dB · sim vs measured: 67–89 vs 70–91 dB

  • Materials screened by TL at 315 Hz: aluminium, ACP, steel, magnesium, carbon steel, LDPE, HDPE
  • Mechanical parameters studied: Young, shear & bulk modulus, Poisson ratio, density
  • Two device geometries validated against real measurements (EOAS 2: sim 64–83 dB vs measured 64–86 dB)
  • UNPAD Physics, 2023 — final thesis project
Heat TransferMultiphysics

ICP Torch Thermal Model for Tungsten Carbide Processing

2D axisymmetric thermal–fluid model of an inductively coupled plasma quartz-glass torch heating binderless tungsten carbide, replicating the setup of Peng Lyu (2023) with a custom Gaussian heat source.

Operating map 0.4–1 kW → peak T 2,622–4,046 K

  • Explicit Gaussian source: (P_RF/P_ref)·Q_peak·exp(−r²/2σ_r²)·exp(−(z−z₀)²/2σ_z²)
  • Domains: argon gas, quartz glass torch wall, external air, WC sample plane (0.5–2 mm standoff)
  • Gaussian fitting of temperature cut-lines per power level; calibrated h workflow
  • Delivers power-optimization insight and reduces physical experiment cost · 2025
Heat TransferBioheatVideo

Bioheat Transfer in 3-Layer Human Skin

Steady-state bioheat transfer model of layered human skin (epidermis, dermis, subcutaneous fat) in COMSOL, capturing the thermal gradient from a cooled 32.4°C surface down to the 37°C body core.

Surface 32.4°C → core 37°C across epidermis / dermis / fat

  • Three tissue layers: epidermis (0.1 mm), dermis (1.5 mm), fat (3 mm)
  • Superficial tissues cool rapidly under external convective flux; deep fat stays near core temperature, insulated by blood perfusion and conduction
  • Demonstrates COMSOL Bioheat Transfer interface — relevant to medical-device and thermal-therapy work
  • Published as an engineering walkthrough on LinkedIn ↓

StructuralCode ComplianceReal Structure

Concrete Vehicle Platform — Structural Safety FEA

3D slab–column–foundation model in COMSOL Solid Mechanics evaluating a real reinforced concrete parking platform under the rear-axle load of a Toyota Hiace (20,601 N applied on tire contact patches).

Deflection 0.38 mm ≪ 9.7 mm limit · tensile 1.79 < 2.56 MPa

  • 13.175 m² × 0.15 m slab, four 15×30 cm columns, ring & ground beams, pad footings
  • Dead load via body load (−g·ρ); serviceability checked at L/350
  • Interpreted against Eurocode 2 and SNI 2847:2019 — flexural safety ratio ≈ 1.5
  • Conclusion: safe, no reinforcement modification required · 2025

IoTMetrologyConcept

IoT-Automated Gravimetric Master Meter Testing

Conceptual prototype for automating fuel master-meter verification by the gravimetric method with IoT monitoring — replacing a slow, two-operator volumetric workflow at the Directorate of Metrology. An ESP32 brain reads flow, temperature and pressure sensors and drives a pump and solenoid valve.

Cuts operators from two to one · improves flowmeter data consistency

  • System brain: ESP32 microcontroller managing measurement logic and wireless data transmission
  • Sensor circuit: integrated water-flow, temperature and pressure sensors — eliminates manual recording
  • Actuator: circulation pump + normally-closed solenoid valve for precise liquid-volume control
  • Mandated by PERMENDAG RI No. 52/2019 (transition to gravimetric method); also OIML R117-1, API MPMS 12.2
  • Internship at Directorate of Metrology, Ministry of Trade (KEMENDAG), 2022 — presented to officials with positive feedback
